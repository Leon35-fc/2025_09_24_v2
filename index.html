Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will Become your new favoeirw holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For topping:

  • 3 medium yellow
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 1o ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife id desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingridients untile the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
